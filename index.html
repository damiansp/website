I am a professional consultant with many years' experience providing businesses, governments, and academics with data-driven solutions to their problems.

My area of expertise is in statistics and mathematical modeling, as well as in generating agent-based models (or simulations). I have professional experience working in social sciences, biology, and natural resource management, and with municipal govenments with land use and energy problems. My skills can help businesses to analyze different businesss strategies, compare efficiency of different equipment or management strategies, compare financial strategies or predict future revenue. For nearly any question that can be asked, statistics can be used to provide better answers and better solutions.

Available statistical and machine learning methods include a wide variety of models such as:

  • Generalized Linear Models (GLM)
  • Non-Parametric Regression
  • Nonlinear Regression
  • Generalized Additive Models (GAM)
  • Principal Components Analysis (PCA)
  • Time Series (ARIMA, GARCH, etc.)
  • Kernel-based smoothing methods
Machine learning methods, for both supervised and unsupervised learning including:
  • Regression methods
  • Classification and Regression Trees
  • K-means, Nearest Neighnor, and various other clustering techniques
  • Naive Bayes
  • Neural Networks
  • Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Market Basket Analysis
  • Recommender Systems (e.g., like Netflix or Amazon uses to make recommendations)

I also provide data visualization services to help better communicate and understand patterns in data, and GIS, web mapping, and various services for web development.

I am based in Eugene, Oregon, but am available for contract anywhere in the world.
